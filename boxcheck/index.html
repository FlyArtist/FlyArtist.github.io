BoxCheck for Radio & Checkbox v1.0.0

Making Radio & Checkbox into a box or card with Pure CSS and No Javascript!

Quick Start

In a browser:

<link rel="stylesheet" href="boxcheck.css">
<div class="box-check-group">
    <label class="box-check">
        <input type="radio" name="radio-outline-default" class="box-check-input" checked>
        <div class="box-check-label">checked</div>
    </label>
    <label class="box-check">
        <input type="radio" name="radio-outline-default" class="box-check-input">
        <div class="box-check-label">normal</div>
    </label>
    <label class="box-check">
        <input type="radio" name="radio-outline-default" class="box-check-input" disabled>
        <div class="box-check-label">disabled</div>
    </label>
</div>
